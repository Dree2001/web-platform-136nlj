Work at JavaJam

Cafe Assistant

As a Cafe Assistant, you will support the daily operations of our coffee shop and help create a welcoming atmosphere for our customers. Your responsibilities will include:

  • Greeting and assisting customers with menu choices and inquiries.
  • Preparing and serving pastries, sandwiches, and other cafe snacks.
  • Keeping the cafe area clean and organized, including busing tables and dishwashing.
  • Handling customer orders and payments with a friendly and efficient demeanor.

Hourly Wage: $11.00

Barista

As a Barista at our coffee shop, you will be responsible for creating exceptional coffee and espresso beverages, providing excellent customer service, and maintaining a clean and welcoming environment for our customers.

  • Prepare and serve a variety of coffee and espresso drinks.
  • Operate coffee machines and equipment with precision and care.
  • Handle customer transactions and cash accurately.
  • Restock coffee beans, syrups, and other supplies as needed.

Hourly Wage: $12.00
